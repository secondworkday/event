TORQworks respects the privacy of every individual who visits our web site or interacts with us in any way. This Privacy Policy states how TORQworks will collect information and use it appropriately.

Personal Information

There are several opportunities on our web site for you to provide us with personal information about you and your interests, such as your name, mailing address and e-mail address. If you choose to share any personal information with us, we may store it and use it for internal marketing research and other marketing purposes. We do not sell, license or disclose your personal information, or information that may potentially identify you, to other companies.TORQworks will not sell, license or disclose such information to others outside of our company unless (a) expressly authorized by you, (b) necessary to enable our contractors or agents to perform certain functions for us, (c) necessary for us to protect our interests or those of our clients, or (d) required by law or to comply with the legal process, such as responding to a subpoena or court order. In all cases, we will disclose the information consistent with applicable laws and regulations and we will require the recipient to protect the information and use it only for the purpose it was provided. We use personal information you provide to us in order to respond to your requests or to provide you with products or services you may request. If you choose to post a comment on the TORQworks website, your name (or screen alias) and any other personal information you choose to provide may be displayed on the web site.

Non-Identifying Information

When you visit our web site, we may also collect information or data from you that do not identify you personally. Examples of this information include the length and time of your visit, the pages you looked at on our site, the type of Internet browser you have and the site you visited just before coming to ours. We use this information to develop ideas for improving our site and to measure site activity.We may disclose this non-identifying information in aggregate form to others for such purposes.

Cookies

Our web site makes use of 'cookie' files to customize information to your personal preferences or interests. A cookie is an element of data that a web site can send to your browser, which may store some information on your computer. Then, when you come back to our site, we can tailor information to suit your individual preferences. The goal is to save you time and enhance your experience in using our web site. Most Internet browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer hard drive, block all cookies or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please refer to your browser instructions.

Surveys

We request your input to evaluate potential features and services for TORQworks. The decision to be involved is completely yours.From time to time, we may also ask you to respond to surveys or questionnaires, but your involvement is completely voluntary. We do not employ special tools or try to find any e-mail addresses that are not provided to us by you.

However, TORQworks is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third party web sites that may be linked to this site. These links are meant for the user's convenience only and do not constitute sponsorship, endorsement or approval by TORQworks of the content, policies or practices of such sites nor any products or services which may be offered through that site. Once you have left the TORQworks site via such a link, you should check the applicable privacy policy of the third party site.

Children

If a minor has provided us with personal information without the consent of a parent or guardian, a parent or guardian of that minor should contact us at info@torqworks.com, to request that we delete this information from our files.
